## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account

Craftwell supports three ways to sign up:

- **Google OAuth** -- Click "Sign in with Google" on the login page. You will be redirected to Google to authorize the app, then sent back to Craftwell automatically.
- **Phone Login** -- Enter your phone number to receive a one-time passcode via SMS. Enter the code to complete sign-in.
- **Email and Password** -- Enter your email and choose a password. You may need to verify your email address before continuing.

After signing in for the first time, you will be directed to the onboarding survey.

### Returning Users

If you already have an account, use the same sign-in method you used during registration. You will be taken directly to the Protocols page.

## 2. Onboarding Survey

The onboarding survey helps Craftwell personalize your experience. It consists of four steps:

1. **Health Goals** -- Select the areas you want to improve (e.g., sleep, focus, energy, stress, fitness).
2. **Current Habits** -- Rate your sleep quality (1-10), exercise frequency, and stress level (1-10).
3. **Supplement Experience** -- Indicate your familiarity with supplements (none, some, experienced).
4. **Focus Areas** -- Choose the specific routines you are most interested in (e.g., morning routine, cold exposure, sleep optimization).

You can update your survey answers later from the Profile page. Your responses influence protocol recommendations and AI chat context.

## 3. Browsing Protocols

After onboarding, you land on the **Protocols** page, which lists all available health protocols organized by category.

### Categories

Protocols are grouped by category (e.g., Light, Temperature, Supplements, Exercise, Sleep, Focus). Use the category tabs at the top to filter the list.

### Search

Use the search bar to find protocols by name or description. Results are ranked by effectiveness.

### Filters

- **Category tabs** -- Show only protocols in a specific category.
- **Favorites** -- Toggle the favorites filter to see only protocols you have bookmarked.
- **Your Protocols** -- View only the protocols you have added to your personal stack.

### Favoriting

Tap the heart icon on any protocol card to add it to your favorites. Tap again to remove it. Favorites are saved to your account and persist across devices.

## 4. Protocol Details

Tap on any protocol card to open its detail page. Each protocol includes:

### Overview

- **Title and category** -- The protocol name and which category it belongs to.
- **Effectiveness rank** -- A numerical ranking within its category based on evidence strength.
- **Difficulty level** -- Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced.
- **Time commitment** -- Estimated daily time required.
- **Description** -- A summary of what the protocol involves and its benefits.

### Tools (Steps)

Each protocol has one or more tools, which are the specific actions you perform daily. Each tool shows:
- A title and description of what to do.
- Implementation instructions with details on how to perform the step correctly.

### References

Links to the original Huberman Lab podcast episodes or newsletters where this protocol was discussed.

### Actions

- **Add to My Protocols** -- Adds the protocol to your personal stack for daily tracking.
- **Favorite** -- Bookmarks the protocol for quick access.
- **Notes** -- Write personal notes about the protocol (up to 5,000 characters). Use this to track observations, modifications, or reminders.
- **Share** -- Share the protocol via a deep link. The shared link includes Open Graph metadata for rich previews on social media.

## 5. Daily Tracking

Once you have added protocols to your stack, you can track your daily completions.

### Completing Tools

On the protocol detail page or in your protocol stack view, each tool has a checkbox. Tap the checkbox to mark a tool as completed for today. Tap again to un-complete it (toggle behavior).

Completions are date-aware and respect your local timezone. Each day resets at midnight in your timezone.

### Streaks

Craftwell tracks your consistency with streaks:

- **Current streak** -- The number of consecutive days you have fully completed all tools in a protocol (every single tool must be checked).
- **Longest streak** -- Your all-time best consecutive run.
- **Total days** -- The total number of days you have completed the protocol.

A day counts as "complete" only if you finish every tool in the protocol. Partial completions do not extend your streak.

### Exporting Data

You can export your entire completion history as a CSV file from the Profile page. The export includes the date, protocol name, tool name, and completion status for every recorded day.

## 6. Dashboard

The **Dashboard** provides a weekly overview of your adherence across all active protocols.

### Weekly View

- The dashboard shows a 7-day grid (Monday through Sunday) for each active protocol.
- Each cell displays a percentage showing how many tools you completed out of the total for that protocol on that day.
- Fully completed days are highlighted.

### Navigation

- Use the arrow buttons to move between weeks.
- The current week is shown by default.

### Overall Adherence

A summary percentage at the top shows your combined adherence across all protocols for the displayed week.

## 7. AI Chat

The AI Chat feature lets you ask health-related questions grounded in Huberman Lab research.

### Starting a Conversation

Navigate to the Chat page and type your question. The AI will respond using information from the Huberman Lab knowledge base, citing specific podcast episodes and newsletters where applicable.

### Protocol Context

When you open the chat from a specific protocol page, the AI automatically receives context about that protocol. This lets you ask targeted questions like "How should I time this supplement?" without needing to specify which protocol you mean.

### Citations and Sources

AI responses include source references linking back to the original Huberman Lab content. These appear as citations within the response, so you can verify the information and explore the primary source material.

### Chat Sessions

- Each conversation is saved as a session with an auto-generated title.
- You can rename sessions from the chat sidebar.
- You can delete sessions you no longer need.
- Use the search feature in the chat sidebar to find messages across all your sessions.

### Rate Limiting

To manage costs, the chat is limited to 20 messages per minute. If you hit the limit, wait a moment before sending your next message.

## 8. Profile and Achievements

### Profile Settings

Access your profile from the navigation menu. From here you can:

- **Edit your name** -- Update your display name, first name, and last name.
- **Update age** -- Keep your age current for relevant recommendations.
- **Review survey answers** -- Re-take or modify your onboarding survey responses.
- **Export data** -- Download your completion history as CSV.
- **Delete account** -- Permanently remove your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

### Achievements

Craftwell includes 12 achievements that reward your engagement. Achievements track milestones such as:

- Adding your first protocol
- Reaching streak milestones (3, 7, 14, 30 days)
- Completing a certain number of total tools
- Having your first AI chat conversation
- Adding multiple protocols to your stack

Each achievement shows your current progress toward the target. Unlocked achievements display with a highlighted icon.
